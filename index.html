About Us

The Elite Warriors of Futsal

We are Wakanda FC, a futsal club forged in the heart of Sri Lanka with a vision of excellence, discipline, and unwavering unity. Founded in 2023, we represent more than just a team—we are a brotherhood bound by fearlessness and elite performance. Every match is a battle, every player a warrior. We don't just compete; we dominate. With precision, power, and relentless determination, we've carved our legacy in the futsal arena. Our motto is simple: Wakanda Forever—united we rise, united we conquer.
